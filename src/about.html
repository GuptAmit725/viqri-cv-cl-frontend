Our Story

Viqri CV was born from a simple observation: talented professionals were being overlooked because their CVs didn't effectively communicate their value. Traditional CV writing services were expensive and time-consuming, making them inaccessible to many job seekers.

We set out to solve this problem by combining the power of AI with years of recruitment expertise. Our platform analyzes thousands of successful CVs to understand what works, then applies that knowledge to help you craft your perfect professional story.

Today, we're proud to serve job seekers across the globe, helping them land interviews at top companies and advance their careers.
